Logistics & Tips

Everything you need to show up ready and get the most out of your time in Cancun.

Morning Routine

  • Eat breakfast before 9am. The resort buffet and restaurants open early — fuel up before sessions begin.
  • Coffee, tea, sodas, and sweet breads will be waiting for you at Roja 1 & 2.
  • Sessions run 9am–1pm sharp. Plan your mornings accordingly.
  • Afternoons are wide open — pool, beach, or 1:1 connections with fellow CEOs.

What to Bring

  • Your biggest business challenge. Come prepared to share and get hot seats.
  • Notebook & pen — or your preferred digital note-taking setup.
  • Resort casual for sessions; swimwear for afternoons.
  • An open mind and a willingness to be honest about where you're stuck.

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Arrival: Check in any time on or before Tuesday, April 14.
  • Welcome Reception: Tuesday, April 14 at 6pm — The Boardwalk. Don't miss it.
  • Check-out: Saturday, April 18. All rooms out by standard checkout time.
  • Contact the resort directly to arrange late checkout or luggage storage if you have an afternoon flight.

Getting There

  • Fly into Cancún International Airport (CUN) — approximately 20 minutes from the resort.
  • Uber, private transfer, or taxi to Blvd. Kukulcán Km. 14.5 in the Hotel Zone.
  • The resort is adults-only and all-inclusive — food, drinks, and activities are included.
  • Tipping in local currency (pesos) is appreciated and customary for resort staff.
